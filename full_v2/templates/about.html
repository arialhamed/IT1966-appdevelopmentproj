THE QUEENSWAY SHOPPING CENTRE STORY


Queensway Shopping Centre is one of Singapore’s most popular malls for sports apparels. It offers a wide range of sports gear, limited edition sports apparels and equipment. Since its opening in 1976, Queensway Shopping Centre has become well known over the time as ‘Singapore’s sports mall’.

The mall also features a unique mix of specialty services such as leather smith, embroidery design and print, to laser engraving. Being one of the oldest malls in Singapore, Queensway Shopping Centre offers shoppers a blend of both modern and nostalgic shopping experience like no others.

Located in close proximity to Queenstown MRT Station (EW19), it is also a favourite hangout for families and youths.
